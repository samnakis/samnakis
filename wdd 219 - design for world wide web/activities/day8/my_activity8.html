Polar Vortex Strikes Indiana

This is a simple text file that was created on February 17th, 2021. It was a cold day outside. All the H20 was frozen. Chris Braun, a local Hoosier, had difficulty digging his car out of the snow. "It was an ordeal", said Braun, "I had to shovel off my 20 × 8 parking spot. That’s 160 ft2 man! I would pay £10 for someone to do that! I would pay more, but I don’t wear Dolce & Gabbana if you know what I’m saying."

A weary Braun then returned to work writing a webpage for his sister’s recording company <Super Art> ™.
